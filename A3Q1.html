7 Wonders of the worlds

The Seven Wonders of the World include iconic landmarks: the Great Wall of China, Petra in Jordan, Christ the Redeemer in Brazil, the Colosseum in Italy, the Taj Mahal in India, Machu Picchu in Peru, and Chichén Itzá in Mexico. These wonders are renowned for their architectural brilliance and historical significance.
